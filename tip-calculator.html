How to calculate a tip

To calculate a tip, multiply your bill amount by the tip percentage and divide by 100. Then add the tip to the original bill to get the total amount to pay.

For example, a 15% tip on a €50 bill is €7.50, bringing the total to €57.50. This tip calculator is helpful in restaurants, cafes, taxis, or any service where gratuity is customary.
