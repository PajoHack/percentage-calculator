How to calculate a discount

To calculate a discount, multiply the original price by the discount percentage and divide by 100. Then subtract that value from the original price to get the final price after the discount.

For example, if an item costs €100 and the discount is 25%, you save €25 and pay €75. This discount calculator helps you work out sale prices during Black Friday, end-of-season sales, or everyday shopping.
